What is Shark Finning?

Shark finning in the practice of cutting off the fins of sharks (any species) for selling and eating. After the fins or cut the bodies are thrown back into the water to suffocate and die. Many fisherman participate because of the market value of shark fins. The prices range between $400 for sharks and $10,000 - $20,000 for throphy sharks like basking and whale sharks.

The obvious problem is the dramatic changes in shark population which makes the ecosyestems dangerously off balance, with over 100 million or more sharks globally being killed by shark finning. Shark finning is also illegal in many counties, U.S included. This doesn't slow the industry. In addition to it harming the ecosystems it also harms humans, how?
